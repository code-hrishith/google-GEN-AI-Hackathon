ManoHeal aims to provide safe, stigma-free mental health support for students and campus communities. It offers:

  • AI Empathy Chatbot - Emily — for 24/7 psychological first aid and coping strategies.

3. Privacy & Data Protection

ManoHeal is designed with maximum anonymity:

  • Mood trackers, and analytics are stored anonymously and cannot be linked to you unless an emergency escalation is triggered.
  • We never sell or rent your data.
